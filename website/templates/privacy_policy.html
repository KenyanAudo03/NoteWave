How is my data protected?

We prioritize your data security by using advanced encryption protocols and secure servers to protect your information. We use TLS (Transport Layer Security) to ensure that your data is transmitted securely. Our servers are regularly updated and monitored to safeguard against unauthorized access.

For more details, please review our full Privacy Policy.

Do you share my personal information with third parties?

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. We may share information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, but only under strict confidentiality agreements.

For more details, please see our full Privacy Policy.

Do you support 2FA?

Yes, we support Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to enhance your account security. Follow these steps to enable 2FA:

  1. Go to Settings Page

    Navigate to the settings page from your account dashboard.

  2. Select Privacy Section

    Click on the 'Privacy Setting' section in the settings menu.

  3. Enable 2FA

    Click on the 'Enable 2FA' option. You will be given two methods to set up 2FA.

    • Using an Authenticator App

      Scan the QR code displayed on the screen using an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy.

    • Using Email

      Select the email option, and an OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your registered email address. Enter this OTP to complete the setup.

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team.
