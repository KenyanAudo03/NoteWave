Help & Support

Welcome to the NoteWave Help page! Here you will find answers to common questions and tips to help you get the most out of our note-taking application.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I create a new note?

To create a new note, click on the "+" button in your dashboard. You can then enter your content and save it for later.

2. Can I organize my notes into folders?

Yes! You can create Tags to organize your notes by clicking on the New Tag tab and selecting "Create New Tag".

3. Is my data secure?

Absolutely! Your notes are securely encrypted and stored in our cloud. We take privacy seriously and adhere to strict security measures.

4. How do I share a note with someone?

To share a note, open the note you want to share and click the "Share" button. You can then enter the email address of the person you want to share with. Additionally, you can export your notes by navigating to the Settings section. In the Data section, select "Export" to save your notes, and you can also import them back into the app later.

5. What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

Tips for Using NoteWave

  • Use tags to categorize your notes for easier searching.
  • Regularly back up your notes to avoid data loss.
  • Explore our templates for quick note-taking.
  • Utilize the search bar to find specific notes quickly.
  • Set reminders for important notes to stay organized and on track.
  • Take advantage of keyboard shortcuts to speed up your note-taking process.
  • Customize your notes with colors and fonts for better visual organization.
  • Share your notes with friends or colleagues for collaborative projects.
  • Use the checklist feature for task management within your notes.
  • Regularly review and clean up your notes to keep them organized.
